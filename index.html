Welcome to HERO Ops

A live, fully interactive demo of the HERO Ops safety-management platform — seeded with a fictional operator (Canyon County Air Rescue). Nothing here is real data, and nothing you do is saved: refresh and it resets.
1
Sign in as any crew member. Pick Joel Smith (Chief Pilot / manager) or K. Whitfield (Safety Officer) to see every feature — including the risk matrix, Safety Health analytics, and personnel & fleet management.
2
Everything is clickable. File a FRAT and watch it auto-score, log a flight, file a hazard report with photos, run a preflight, check anyone's currency, condition the hoist. The tabs across the top are the whole platform.
3
On a phone? Make it feel like a native app: in Safari tap Share → Add to Home Screen; in Chrome on Android tap ⋮ → Add to Home screen. It launches full-screen from your home screen.
